ABOUT US


Cognitus Asia Sdn. Bhd. was first established in 2006 as eCTL Technologies Sdn Bhd (720630-T) with over 40 employees. We are a team of experts with expertise in providing business intelligence and data management related consulting services and project management with vast experience and knowledge in different infrastructure setups and environments both locally and globally. We offer all-rounded data related services such as customized business intelligence, statistical analysis, report automation and database management projects along with managing and analyzing your data for auditing and reporting purposes.


"At Cognitus Asia Sdn. Bhd., we believe that your success reflects on our own accomplishments in providing you with quality data-related services. We are fully committed and dedicated to our work when your business benefitted and grew with the assistance of our efforts."

- Founder, Mak

OUR SERVICES


Our clienteles are among organizations throughout the Asia Pacific region including Malaysia, Hong Kong, Thailand, Singapore and Dubai. Our goal is to expand the border where we can offer our services such as;



Extraction, cleansing, transformation & loading process for Data Warehouse/Data Mart

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) implementations
